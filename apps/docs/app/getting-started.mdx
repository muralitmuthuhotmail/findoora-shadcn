# Getting Started

Welcome to Findoora! This guide will help you get up and running with the application quickly.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- A stable internet connection
- An email address for account registration

## Creating Your Account

## Creating Your Account

Follow these steps to create your Findoora account:

### 1. Visit the Findoora Website

Navigate to the Findoora application and click on the "Sign Up" button.

### 2. Fill in Your Details

Provide the following information:
- **Email Address**: Your primary email for account verification
- **Password**: Choose a strong password (minimum 8 characters)
- **Name**: Your first and last name

### 3. Verify Your Email

Check your email inbox for a verification message from Findoora and click the verification link.

### 4. Complete Your Profile

Once verified, log in and complete your profile with additional information that will help personalize your experience.

## First Steps After Registration

### 1. Explore the Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see the main dashboard. Take a moment to familiarize yourself with:

- **Navigation menu**: Located in the sidebar or top bar
- **Search functionality**: The primary feature for finding content
- **User settings**: Access your account preferences

### 2. Set Up Your Preferences

Configure your account settings:

```
Settings → Preferences → General
```

Key settings to configure:
- **Language**: Choose your preferred language
- **Notifications**: Set up email and in-app notifications
- **Privacy**: Configure your privacy preferences

### 3. Start Using Search

The main feature of Findoora is its powerful search capability:

1. Use the search bar at the top of the page
2. Enter keywords related to what you're looking for
3. Use filters to narrow down results
4. Save searches for future reference
