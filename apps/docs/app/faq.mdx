# Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Findoora.

## General Questions

### What is Findoora?

Findoora is a powerful content discovery and organization platform that helps users find, organize, and collaborate on digital content. It combines advanced search capabilities with intuitive organization tools to help you manage information more effectively.

### How much does Findoora cost?

We offer several pricing tiers:
- **Free**: Basic features for individual users
- **Pro** ($9/month): Advanced features and increased limits
- **Team** ($25/month): Collaboration features and team management
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for large organizations

### Is my data secure?

Yes, security is our top priority. We use:
- End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
- SOC 2 Type II compliance
- Regular security audits
- GDPR and CCPA compliance

3. Enter your email address

Yes, you can update your email address in your account settings:

2. Click "Change Email"
3. Enter your new email address

## Using Findoora

### How do I search for content?

Findoora offers several ways to search:

**Basic Search:**
- Type your query in the search bar
- Use natural language phrases
- Results appear instantly as you type

**Advanced Search:**
- Use quotes for exact phrases: `"project management"`
- Filter by type: `type:pdf`, `type:image`
- Search by date: `date:2024`, `date:last-week`
- Site-specific: `site:github.com`

### Can I collaborate with others?

Yes! Findoora offers robust collaboration features:

- **Shared Workspaces**: Create team spaces with role-based access
- **Content Sharing**: Share individual items or entire collections
- **Comments & Annotations**: Discuss content with team members
- **Real-time Updates**: See changes from team members instantly

### How do I invite team members?

1. Go to your workspace settings
2. Click "Invite Members"
3. Enter email addresses
4. Choose permission levels (Viewer, Editor, Admin)
5. Send invitations

## Technical Questions

### What browsers are supported?

Findoora works on all modern browsers:
- Chrome 90+
- Firefox 88+
- Safari 14+
- Edge 90+

### Is there a mobile app?

Yes, we have mobile apps for:
- iOS (iPhone and iPad)
- Android (phones and tablets)

Download from the App Store or Google Play Store.

### Can I use Findoora offline?

Limited offline functionality is available:
- Previously viewed content is cached for offline access
- Search history and saved items are available offline
- New searches require an internet connection

### How do I export my data?

You can export your data in several formats:

1. Go to Settings → Data Export
2. Choose what to export (all content, specific collections, etc.)
3. Select format (JSON, CSV, PDF)
4. Download the export file

## Integrations

### What integrations are available?

Findoora integrates with many popular tools:

**Cloud Storage:**
- Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive
- Box, iCloud Drive

**Productivity:**
- Gmail, Outlook, Slack
- Notion, Trello, Asana
- Microsoft Teams, Zoom

**Developer Tools:**
- GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket
- Jira, Confluence

### How do I set up integrations?

1. Go to Settings → Integrations
2. Find the service you want to connect
3. Click "Connect" and authorize access
4. Configure sync preferences

### Can I disconnect integrations?

Yes, you can disconnect any integration:
1. Go to Settings → Integrations
2. Find the connected service
3. Click "Disconnect"
4. Confirm the action

Note: Disconnecting won't delete already imported content.

## Troubleshooting

### Search isn't returning expected results

Try these troubleshooting steps:

<Callout type="info" emoji="🔍">
**Search Tips:**
- Use simpler, more general terms
- Check for typos in your query

### The app is loading slowly

Performance issues can be caused by:
- Slow internet connection
- Browser cache issues (try clearing cache)
- Too many browser tabs open
- Browser extensions interfering

### I can't log in

If you're having login issues:
1. Check that you're using the correct email and password
2. Try resetting your password
3. Clear your browser cookies and cache
4. Try logging in from a different browser
5. Contact support if issues persist

### Content isn't syncing

If content isn't syncing across devices:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try refreshing the page or restarting the app
3. Verify you're logged into the same account
4. Check if you've reached storage limits

## Billing & Subscriptions

### How do I upgrade my plan?

1. Go to Settings → Billing
2. Click "Upgrade Plan"
3. Choose your new plan
4. Enter payment information
5. Confirm the upgrade

### Can I downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can downgrade at any time:
1. Go to Settings → Billing
2. Click "Change Plan"
3. Select a lower-tier plan
4. Confirm the change (takes effect at the next billing cycle)

### How do I cancel my subscription?

1. Go to Settings → Billing
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Choose a reason (optional)
4. Confirm cancellation

Your account will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
